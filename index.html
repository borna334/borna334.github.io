The day I nearly died

July 12th 2019

Today I walked next to the river when I nearly steped on a snake. I stopped immediatly.The snake's fangs went in and out.
I was terrified. I didn't move. One bite and you're dead in 3 hours.

Knives and guns!

September 10th 2019

Early this morning we crossed the river by boat when we saw five canoes. The tribesmen carried knives and guns.
They were very angry because we didn't have premission to be on their land. We left as fast as we could.

The jungle night

November 24th 2019

I lied in my hammock last night trying to sleep, but it was impossible because the noise of the jungle was so loud.
Monkeys screamed in the trees, and millions of mosquitos buzzed around my head. I took a sleeping pill and finally fell asleep at 3 am.
